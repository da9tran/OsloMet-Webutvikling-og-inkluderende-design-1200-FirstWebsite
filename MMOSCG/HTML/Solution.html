Our solution

The solution we propose, is an app called SelfLearn. SelfLearn has a variety of games that teach children subject such as mathematics or science through a fun engaging way.


In addition SelfLearn comes with different tiers of diffeculty letting the children challenge themselves with harder question. The app encourages the user to use these tiers of diffeculty and competion through leaderboards and highscore list in which higher diffeculty rewards more points.
